Epiphany Nursery and Primary School.

Overview

Epiphany schools accepts children from two and a half year old into the Early Years Foundation Stage (Nursery), then they progress through kindergarten then primary school where they study until time for their secondary education.
School Times

  • ▪ Studies start at - 8.45am - 3pm
  • ▪ Reception - 8.45am - 3pm
  • ▪ Whole school assemblies are at 8am on Mondays to Fridays.

Curriculum

When children move into school, they follow the epiphany curriculum. This is based on the National Curriculum, but often exceeds those expectations. Children are taught in 3 classes, with two year groups making up each class. Maths and English are taught separately to each year groups but the other subjects follow a two year rolling programme. Please see the documents on the relevant website pages, to get an idea of what the children study in the foundation subjects.

Postaral Care

Pastoral care is our priority and is reflected and embedded in all our aspects of nursery life. We aim to give our boys the best start to their education by creating a secure and happy learning environment. Our pastoral care develops skills for life as boys learn to be part of a group while retaining their individuality.

  • ▪ We are gentle
  • ▪ We are kind and helpful
  • ▪ We listen
  • ▪ We share and take turns
  • ▪ We are polite
